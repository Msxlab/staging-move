## Executive summary

Partnership monetization is promising, but it must be disclosed, consented, compliance-safe, and framed as guided warm hand-offs. The product should not claim live partnerships, official affiliations, traffic, conversion, or revenue until those are verified.

## Strategic interpretation

Priority partnership categories from the Claude handoff:

1. Broadband/ISP affiliate or referral.
2. Self-storage marketplace.
3. Moving lead/booking marketplace.
4. Utility concierge.
5. Property managers / multifamily.
6. Real-estate brokerages / agents.
7. Insurance agencies or brokers with flat non-contingent referral only.
8. Mortgage/title.
9. Relocation/employer/military/senior/student housing.
10. Address-validation/data vendors as suppliers.

## Hypotheses

- Hypothesis: broadband referral is the first clean external revenue test because serviceability is already part of the move moment.
- Hypothesis: storage and mover hand-offs can monetize explicit intent without turning LocateFlow into a spammy lead funnel.
- Hypothesis: B2B2C partner channels are larger but should wait for consumer pull and proof.

## Recommended decisions

- Use consented, disclosed click-outs before any PII handoff.
- Do not show partner logos, official affiliation, or marketplace claims before signed agreements.
- Defer partner-facing workspace until at least one verified partner actively asks for it.
- Keep partnership experiments additive to the subscription ladder.

## Validation needed

- Confirm affiliate approvals, terms, payout models, disclosure requirements, and data-sharing limits.
- Validate user trust and conversion impact before scaling referral surfaces.
- Confirm legal review for insurance, SMS, and lead handoff copy.
