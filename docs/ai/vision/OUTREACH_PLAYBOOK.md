## Hypotheses

- Hypothesis: partners care most about movers with date, destination context, and explicit intent.
- Hypothesis: a demo around real address intelligence, transition plan, proof packet, and monitoring is more compelling than generic lead volume.
- Hypothesis: the safest first offer is a disclosed click-out with no PII handoff.

## Recommended decisions

- Do not claim partnerships in product or marketing until verified.
- Use outreach to validate appetite, compliance requirements, data-sharing constraints, and partner economics.
- Keep outreach scripts grounded in current capability and "guided action" language.

## Validation needed

- Whether any partner will actively distribute or pay.
- Whether users trust partner click-outs inside a move command center.
