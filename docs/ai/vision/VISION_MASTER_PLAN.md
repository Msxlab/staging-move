## Executive summary

LocateFlow should evolve as the Address Life OS / Move Command Center: one trusted place for households to organize, understand, monitor, and prove every address-linked obligation before, during, and after a move.

The Claude vision handoff argues that the next year should not be spent only "integrating APIs." Reported verified source-integration work suggests much of the address-intelligence layer is already wired; spot-check recommended before treating each integration claim as independently verified in this task. The bigger opportunity is packaging, monetization, trust positioning, measurement, and guided action over existing Home Dossier, AI Move Briefing, Provider Transition, post-move monitoring, and export/proof primitives.

## Strategic interpretation

- Trust is the key positioning wedge: "guided action" rather than "we do it for you."
- Premium value should be framed as intelligence, proof, and continuity.
- Referral and partner monetization should be additive to subscription, not the core product promise.
- SEO/GEO should use source-backed tools and guides based on dossier/rules data, not thin programmatic pages.
- The product should avoid becoming a lead-generation funnel or data broker.

## Hypotheses

- Hypothesis: the personalized briefing/dossier on a real address creates the strongest first-session aha.
- Hypothesis: post-move monitoring solves the episodic-move retention problem.
- Hypothesis: proof packets create higher willingness to pay than generic checklist features.
- Hypothesis: consumer activation and paid conversion should be validated before B2B2C or partner-facing workspace work.

## Recommended decisions

- Keep Address Life OS / Move Command Center as the master narrative.
- Prioritize monetizing and surfacing already-built intelligence before adding many new integrations.
- Use guided-action language across provider, USPS, insurance, and partner workflows unless direct automation is independently verified.
- Treat trust, disclosure, consent, and no-raw-PII data design as product differentiators.
- Defer live partnership, provider automation, and SEO scale claims until verified.
