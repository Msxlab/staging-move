## Executive summary

The proposed plan sequences trust, monetization, and validation before broad partner or SEO scale. The first 90 days should prove activation, paid intent, one free-tool loop, disclosed referral interest, post-move retention, and transition-outcome data capture.

## Strategic interpretation

0-90 days:

- Ship approved UX experiments.
- Instrument Individual paywall and activation funnel.
- Launch one free tool that saves to account.
- Ship USPS COA anti-scam guide.
- Test broadband referral click-out with disclosure.
- Start transition-outcome graph v1 and 5-state rules dataset.

90-180 days:

- Package post-move monitoring.
- Add proof-packet preview and e-sign prototype.
- Publish a small number of source-backed state-rule guides.
- Explore storage and mover referral tests.
- Begin property-manager pilot conversations without building partner workspace.

180-365 days:

- Make monitoring a standing subscription reason.
- Expand Move Rules Registry/GEO if source workflow works.
- Evaluate utility referral substrate.
- Evaluate B2B2C only if consumer metrics and partner pull are real.
- Begin aggregated non-PII insights R&D only after privacy gates.

## Hypotheses

- Hypothesis: the 0-90 day work should prove money and trust before platform expansion.
- Hypothesis: post-move monitoring is the retention bridge from episodic move app to Address Life OS.
- Hypothesis: partner/B2B scale should wait for consumer proof.

## Recommended decisions

- Choose one growth/vision lane before source work.
- Keep 0-90 day scope focused on validation and trust.
- Require human approval before app source changes, telemetry persistence, billing copy, public rules content, or referral click-outs.

## Validation needed
