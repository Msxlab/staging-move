● My recommendation · V2

Italic L,
warm terracotta.

A rounded chip in the brand's signature terracotta gradient, holding an italic Fraunces L in cream — with a single cream dot above, quietly referencing the "locate" pin. One mark, six uses: app icon, favicon, chip in the nav, seal on receipts, watermark on shared plans, monogram on onboarding.

It reads premium (Fraunces italic + gradient fill), corporate (bounded chip, consistent grid), and warm (terracotta + cream instead of black + white). Pairs naturally with the Fraunces wordmark.
