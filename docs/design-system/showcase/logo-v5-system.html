● The canonical signature

Home to somewhere new.

A warm brass dot for where you are, flowing through a soft arc into a terracotta pin for where you're going. The arc isn't decoration — it's the single frame a user sees every time the app opens, traced in ~1.2s over a quiet gradient. By the time the animation settles, it's also your favicon, your loading spinner, and the seal on any PDF export.

One mark, three moods: traced (hero + splash), breathing (idle / nav), shimmering (sync success).

Motion language

· 4 animated states
signature trace
1.2s path draw on app open, splash, marketing hero. Plays once, holds.
idle breathing
Subtle 4% scale at 3s cycles. For nav logo, any always-on placement.
sync shimmer
Path draws + fades. "Just updated" moments — after transfer, payment, sync.
orbit spinner
Pin orbits on brass track. Loading states. 1.8s per revolution.
