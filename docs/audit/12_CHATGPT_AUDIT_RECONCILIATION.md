The report's highest-value surviving contributions are real but modest: a genuine batch-cap inconsistency (AUD-006/SURF-02), broad but non-urgent test-coverage gaps (AUD-008/SURF-03/SURF-07/SURF-08), authoritative-doc drift (AUD-009), and two residual copy tokens (SURF-04). It contains one outright **inaccurate** claim (SURF-12, a Service "documents" relation/UI that does not exist). Net: useful as a historical baseline and a QA-coverage checklist, but it **missed every serious live defect** that the Claude audit independently confirmed, and its severity ratings should not be trusted without re-verification.
